  • ON CAMPUS

    The university has put into place a series of guidelines in order to keep on-campus learning as safe as possible:

    • Use of the NHS Covid-19 app is mandatory for everybody on campus
    • Buildings on campus have designated exit and entry points
    • Students are advised to use the NHS Covid-19 app to check in to buildings as they enter by scanning the QR code
    • Students must keep 2 metres apart and wear a face covering when indoors
    • Students arriving for in person learning are advised to head straight for their rooms once they enter the building, following directional markings on the floor, in order to help maintain social distancing.
    • Study spaces must be booked in advance

  • TESTING

    In order to help keep students safe, the University is allowing each of it's students to book up to two asymptomatic Covid tests per week.

    Every student at the University is eligible for a test at the University, however they are specifically encouraging students who are required to be on campus to take regular tests in order to reduce the risk of spread.

    Students who plan to return to campus are encouraged to take a single test through NHS test and trace before returning, then to book twice-weekly tests 3-4 days apart for the entire time they are required to work on campus.

    Results will be given to students through the NHS test and trace system, usually within 48 hours.
